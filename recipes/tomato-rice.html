Tomato Rice

Description

Tomato rice is a flavorful South Indian-inspired dish made by cooking rice with ripe tomatoes, aromatic spices, and herbs. It's tangy, mildly spicy, and works perfectly as a quick lunch or dinner option. This one-pot dish is comforting, simple to prepare, and pairs well with raita, pickle, or plain yogurt.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 6 to 8 curry leaves
  • ¼ teaspoon turmeric
  • ½ teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1½ tablespoons oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add mustard seeds, let them pop.
  3. Add curry leaves, garlic, and onion. Sauté until soft.
  4. Add tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook until mushy.
  5. Add cooked rice.
  6. Mix well and cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
  7. Serve hot, optionally with raita or yogurt.
